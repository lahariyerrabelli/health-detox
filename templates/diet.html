Diabetes is a chronic condition in which the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar (glucose). The pancreas either produces little or no insulin, or the body doesn't use insulin effectively.

📋 Types of Diabetes
💉
Type 1 Autoimmune, needs insulin
 🍽️
Type 2 Most common, lifestyle-linked
🤰
Gestational During pregnancy
⚠️
Prediabetes Early warning stage

📊 Blood Sugar Danger Levels
Normal
70–99 mg/dL (Fasting)
Prediabetes
100–125 mg/dL
Diabetes
126+ mg/dL
🚨 Crisis
Below 54 or Above 300 mg/dL
🍽️ Daily Diet Plan
TimeWhat to Eat
🌅 Early MorningWarm water, 5 soaked almonds, flax seeds
🌄 BreakfastOats porridge, vegetable upma, or whole-grain toast with egg

✅❌ Foods to Eat & Avoid

Eat freely:

✅ Spinach✅ Broccoli✅ Oats ✅ Brown Rice✅ Lentils✅ Eggs ✅ Bitter Gourd✅ Fenugreek✅ Almonds
⛔ What Not to Eat

Avoid high-sugar, refined, and fried foods to keep blood sugar steady.

Strictly avoid:

❌ Sugar & Sweets❌ Soft Drinks❌ White Bread ❌ Deep Fried Foods❌ White Rice (excess)❌ Fruit Juices
🏺⚗️ Ancient & Modern Treatment Techniques
🏺 Ancient / Traditional
  • Bitter gourd (karela) juice
  • Fenugreek seed water
  • Turmeric milk (haldi doodh)
  • Ayurvedic herbs: Gurmar, Amla
  • Yoga: Pranayama, Kapalbhati
  • Intermittent fasting (ancient practice)
⚗️ Modern Medical
  • Metformin & other oral medications
  • Insulin therapy (Type 1 & advanced Type 2)
  • Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM)
  • Insulin pump technology
  • Bariatric surgery (severe obesity + T2D)
  • Stem cell research (emerging)
🛡️ Prevention
🏃 Exercise 30 min daily
💧 Stay well hydrated
⚖️ Maintain healthy weight
🚭 Avoid smoking & alcohol
🩺 Regular blood sugar checks
😴 Sleep 7–8 hours/night
🚨 Emergency Warning Signs — Seek Help Immediately
  • Blood sugar below 54 mg/dL — severe hypoglycemia
  • Blood sugar above 300 mg/dL — hyperglycemic crisis
  • Difficulty breathing, fruity breath odor (DKA)
  • Loss of consciousness or extreme confusion
💚 Daily Tip Check blood sugar at the same time daily, take medicines on time, and never skip meals.

Blood pressure is the force exerted by blood against artery walls. Uncontrolled high BP silently damages the heart, brain, kidneys, and blood vessels — often called the "silent killer."

📋 Types / Stages
🟢
Normal Below 120/80
🟡
Elevated 120–129 / <80
🟠
Stage 1 HTN 130–139 / 80–89
🔴
Stage 2 HTN 140/90 or above
🚨
Hypertensive Crisis 180/120+
⬇️
Low BP (Hypotension) Below 90/60
📊 Danger Level Indicators
Normal
Below 120/80 mmHg
Elevated
120–129 / Below 80
Stage 2 HTN
140/90 mmHg +
🚨 Crisis
Above 180/120 mmHg

✅ Banana✅ Spinach✅ Beetroot ✅ Oats✅ Watermelon✅ Garlic ✅ Almonds✅ Walnuts✅ Lentils
⛔ What Not to Eat

Avoid salty, processed, and fatty foods that raise blood pressure.

Strictly avoid:

❌ Excess Salt❌ Pickles❌ Chips & Namkeen ❌ Processed Foods❌ Instant Noodles❌ Alcohol ❌ Sugary Drinks
🏺⚗️ Ancient & Modern Treatment
🏺 Ancient / Traditional
  • Garlic clove on empty stomach
  • Arjuna tree bark decoction (Ayurveda)
  • Hibiscus tea (Roselle flower)
  • Sarpagandha herb (Rauwolfia)
  • Yoga: Shavasana, Anulom-Vilom
  • Low-salt diet (traditional wisdom)
⚗️ Modern Medical
  • ACE inhibitors (e.g., Lisinopril)
  • Beta-blockers & Calcium channel blockers
  • Diuretics (water pills)
  • 24-hour ambulatory BP monitoring
  • Renal denervation (resistant HTN)
  • Lifestyle + medication combo therapy

🛡️ Prevention

🏃 30 min brisk walk daily
⚖️ Maintain healthy BMI
🧘 Yoga & stress management
🚭 Quit smoking
😴 7–8 hours quality sleep
🚨 Emergency Warning Signs
  • BP reading above 180/120 mmHg
  • Severe chest pain or pressure
  • Sudden weakness or numbness on one side
  • Severe headache + blurred vision
  • Difficulty breathing or loss of consciousness
💚 Daily Tip Reduce salt, stay active, monitor BP at home daily, and never stop medication without your doctor's advice.

Fever is a temporary rise in body temperature — usually above 37.2°C — triggered by infection, inflammation, or illness. It's the body's natural immune response to fight pathogens.

📋 Types of Fever
🦠
Viral Fever Most common; flu, cold
🦟
Dengue Fever Aedes mosquito bite
🌊
Typhoid Contaminated food/water
🦟
Malaria Anopheles mosquito
🧫
Bacterial Fever Pneumonia, UTI etc.
🌡️
Heat Stroke Environmental heat
📊 Temperature Danger Levels
Normal
36.1°C – 37.2°C
Mild Fever
37.3°C – 38°C
High Fever
Above 39°C
🚨 Emergency
Above 40°C

⛔ What Not to Eat

Avoid heavy, spicy, and dehydrating foods until fever subsides.

Avoid during fever:

❌ Deep Fried Foods❌ Spicy Food❌ Fast Food ❌ Soft Drinks❌ Heavy Meals❌ Dairy (if nauseated)
🏺⚗️ Ancient & Modern Treatment
🏺 Ancient / Traditional
  • Tulsi + ginger + honey tea
  • Wet cloth on forehead
  • Steam inhalation (eucalyptus)
  • Neem leaves (antimicrobial)
  • Giloy (Tinospora) kadha
  • Adequate rest in warm environment
⚗️ Modern Medical
  • Antipyretics: Paracetamol, Ibuprofen
  • Antibiotics (bacterial causes only)
  • Antivirals (influenza, dengue support)
  • IV fluids for severe dehydration
  • PCR & blood tests for diagnosis
  • Hospitalization for dangerous temps

🛡️ Prevention
🧼 Wash hands frequently
💧 Drink only clean water
🦟 Use mosquito nets/repellents
💉 Stay up to date on vaccines
🥗 Eat hygienic, fresh food
😷 Wear masks in sick environments
🚨 Emergency Warning Signs
  • Temperature above 40°C (104°F)
  • Seizures or convulsions
  • Severe dehydration or persistent vomiting
  • Rash with bleeding spots (dengue sign)
  • Confusion, difficulty breathing, or unconsciousness
💚 Recovery Tip Drink fluids every 30 minutes, rest completely, monitor temperature every 4 hours, and see a doctor if fever persists beyond 3 days.

A headache is pain or discomfort in the head, scalp, or neck. It can range from a minor inconvenience to a sign of serious neurological conditions. Understanding the type helps with proper treatment.

💧

⛔ What Not to Eat

Avoid trigger foods that commonly worsen headache and migraine symptoms.

Avoid these triggers:

❌ Excess Caffeine❌ Alcohol❌ Processed Foods ❌ Aged Cheese❌ MSG (Ajinomoto)❌ Skipping Meals ❌ Artificial Sweeteners
🏺⚗️ Ancient & Modern Treatment
🏺 Ancient / Traditional
  • Ginger + honey tea
  • Peppermint oil on temples
  • Lavender oil aromatherapy
  • Triphala (Ayurvedic herb)
  • Yoga: Balasana, Uttanasana
  • Steam inhalation (sinus headache)
⚗️ Modern Medical
  • Analgesics: Paracetamol, Aspirin
  • Triptans (migraine-specific)
  • CGRP antagonists (new migraine drugs)
  • Preventive: Beta-blockers, Topiramate
  • Botox injections (chronic migraine)
  • MRI/CT scan for recurrent headaches

🛡️ Prevention (Up to 50% of Cancer is Preventable)
🚭 Quit tobacco completely
🏃 Exercise 150 min/week
⚖️ Maintain healthy weight
🩺 Regular cancer screenings
☀️ Sunscreen SPF 30+ daily
💉 HPV & Hepatitis B vaccine
🍷 Limit alcohol intake

🚨 Warning Signs — Never Ignore These
  • Unexplained weight loss of 5+ kg in weeks
  • Lumps or swelling anywhere in the body
  • Persistent cough or blood in phlegm
  • Unusual or unexplained bleeding
  • Severe difficulty swallowing or breathing
  • Changes in moles or skin appearance
💚 Key Message Cancer caught at Stage 0 or Stage I has a survival rate of over 90% for most types. Annual screenings save lives — don't postpone.
