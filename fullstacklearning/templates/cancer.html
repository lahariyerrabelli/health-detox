What is Cancer?

Cancer is a disease in which abnormal cells grow uncontrollably and may spread to different parts of the body. Early detection and treatment can improve outcomes.

Types of Cancer

  • Breast Cancer – Affects breast tissue.
  • Lung Cancer – Affects the lungs.
  • Prostate Cancer – Common in men.
  • Colon Cancer – Affects the large intestine.
  • Skin Cancer – Affects skin cells.
  • Blood Cancer (Leukemia) – Affects blood-forming tissues.
  • Cervical Cancer – Affects the cervix.
  • Oral Cancer – Affects the mouth and throat.

Stages of Cancer

Stage Description
Stage 0 Abnormal cells present but not spread
Stage 1 Small localized cancer
Stage 2 Larger tumor but limited spread
Stage 3 Spread to nearby tissues or lymph nodes
Stage 4 Cancer has spread to distant organs

Warning Signs

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent fatigue
  • Persistent cough
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Unusual bleeding
  • Changes in skin or moles
  • Lumps or swelling

Danger Levels

⚠️ Persistent symptoms lasting several weeks should be evaluated.
🚨 Unusual bleeding, severe weight loss, or difficulty breathing require urgent medical attention.

Risk Factors

  • Smoking and tobacco use
  • Excessive alcohol consumption
  • Unhealthy diet
  • Physical inactivity
  • Obesity
  • Family history of cancer
  • Excessive sun exposure

Prevention

  • Avoid tobacco products.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.
  • Exercise regularly.
  • Eat a balanced diet.
  • Limit alcohol consumption.
  • Attend regular health screenings.
  • Protect skin from excessive sunlight.

Precautions

  1. Attend regular medical checkups.
  2. Follow doctor's treatment plan.
  3. Maintain proper nutrition.
  4. Exercise as advised by healthcare providers.
  5. Avoid smoking and alcohol.
  6. Get adequate sleep.
  7. Manage stress effectively.
  8. Report new symptoms immediately.

Emergency Warning Signs

🚨 Severe difficulty breathing
🚨 Uncontrolled bleeding
🚨 Severe chest pain
🚨 Sudden loss of consciousness
🚨 Severe unexplained weight loss
🚨 Persistent severe pain
💚 Health Tip: A healthy lifestyle, regular screenings, balanced nutrition, and avoiding tobacco are important steps in reducing cancer risk and supporting overall health.
