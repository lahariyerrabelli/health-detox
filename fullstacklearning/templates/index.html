Daily Health Tips

💧
Drink 8–10 Glasses of Water

Proper hydration prevents headaches, fatigue, and kidney stones. Start every morning with a glass.

🥗
Eat a Rainbow of Vegetables

Different coloured vegetables provide different vitamins and antioxidants. Aim for 5 servings daily.

🏃
Exercise 30 Minutes Daily

A brisk 30-minute walk reduces blood pressure, blood sugar, and stress hormones significantly.

😴
Sleep 7–8 Hours Every Night

Poor sleep raises blood pressure and blood sugar. Consistent sleep is free medicine.

🚭
Avoid Tobacco & Excess Alcohol

Smoking causes lung cancer, heart disease, and stroke. Quitting at any age improves health.

🩺
Get Annual Health Checkups

Early detection of diabetes, BP, and cancer improves survival dramatically. Screen regularly.
